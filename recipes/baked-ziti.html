Baked Ziti

Descritpion

Baked ziti is a low-effort lasagna. You get all of the same delicious pasta and cheese combination you love without needing to take time layering noodles, sauce, and cheese. It's also easier on the fingers not grabbing boiled pasta! This recipe is perfect for those who want to go beyond spaghetti but still want an easy recipe. If you can boil pasta and bake something in an oven, then you can master this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ziti or similar pasta
  • 1 (15-oz.) container ricotta cheese
  • 3-4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley
  • 1 (28-oz.) jar pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F). Coat a 13x9 baking dish or 3-quart casserole with cooking spray.
  2. Boil the ziti according to the package directions. Drain and return to pot.
  3. Add ricotta cheese, half the mozzarella, garlic powder, salt, and parsley to the ziti. Mix well.
  4. Spread half the pasta sauce over the bottom of the baking dish. Spoon ziti mixture on top, then cover with the reamining pasta sauce. Sprinkle with the parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake 25 minutes. Cover with the remaining mozzarella cheese, then bake an additional ten minutes.

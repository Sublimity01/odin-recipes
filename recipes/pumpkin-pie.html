Muffin Tin Pumpkin Pies

Description

Pumpkin pie is one of the signature desserts of the fall season. Here we make not a pie, but a tray of miniaturized, individualized pies! Your family and friends will each get their own to enjoy in a single-serve size—although they'll likely go back for a second or third! In addition to being adorable, this also gives more pie crust per bite, complimenting the gooey pumpkin innards.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin pie filling for one 9-inch pie
  • 2 9-inch pie crust doughs
  • Bowl or round cookie cutter 4 inches in diameter
  • Muffin tin
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees (F).
  2. Prep your dough. Using a bowl or cookie cutter, cut out 12 circles from your pie dough.
  3. Place each circle into a pre-greased muffin tin. Press them in, letting the sides come up for a fun look. Score the bottom of your dough to keep it from bubbling up as it cooks.
  4. Prepare your filling according to the package. Pour the filling into each muffin cup, to the very top.
  5. Bake at 425 for 15 minutes. Then turn the heat down to 350 and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes.
  6. Remove each pie from the muffin tin and let cool on a wire rack for a couple of hours to let the filling set.
  7. Add a dollop of whipped cream to each pie and dig in!
